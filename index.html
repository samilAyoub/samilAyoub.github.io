Everything you need to know before choosing a plan

How does the email verification work?

We use AI pattern recognition combined with real-time verification to find and validate work emails. Every email is checked before we show it to you, ensuring 95%+ accuracy. If an email bounces, report it and we'll investigate immediately.

Can I export leads to my CRM?

Yes! LeadFast exports to CSV format which works with any CRM: Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Copper, and more. One-click export, then import directly into your sales system. Growth and Pro plans also include API access for automated workflows.

What makes LeadFast different from Apollo or ZoomInfo?

Three key differences: (1) AI-powered natural language search - just describe who you're looking for in plain English. (2) Better price - we're 50-80% cheaper than competitors. (3) Built for small teams - no enterprise complexity, just fast lead generation.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes. 30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If LeadFast doesn't work for you, just email us and we'll refund every penny. We also offer prorated refunds if you cancel mid-month.

Is this compliant with LinkedIn's terms and GDPR?

Yes. LeadFast uses publicly available data that LinkedIn users have chosen to make public. We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and all data protection regulations. We don't use scraping bots - our AI processes information that's already publicly accessible.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade anytime from your dashboard. Upgrades are immediate, downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle. We'll prorate charges if you upgrade mid-month.
