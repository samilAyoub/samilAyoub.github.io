Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect your email address and name during the checkout process via Stripe. We use this information to create your account and communicate with you regarding your lead reports.

We do not sell your data. Your email is used strictly for product updates, lead delivery, and customer support. We process Reddit data to provide our service but do not store personal information from Reddit users other than their public usernames in your private reports.
